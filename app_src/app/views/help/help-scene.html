How to play (and win)
Tilt your device or use the Q and P buttons to move your paddle left and right. Make sure the ball stays on the screen, else your opponent scores!
Depending on where the ball hits your paddle its return angle and speed will change. Hitting it with the ends will generally increase the difficulty for the other player to catch the ball. And hitting the ball while still moving has extra effect but it comes at the risk of missing.
So, yes, it is very similar to the original version of Pong of 1972 and just as addictive!
Game modes
Classic: the original way of playing Pong with two paddles, one ball and one simple task: getting the ball behind the other player's paddle. Scores are shown but there is no definite end to the game.
Last Paddle Standing: similar to the Classic mode, with one notable change: if your opponent scores your paddle gets smaller and vice versa. The last player to have a piece of paddle left wins.
